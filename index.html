Hao Yu

I am a PhD candidate at the Chair for Computer Aided Medical Procedures & Augmented Reality of Technical University of Munich, supervised of PD Dr. Slobodan Ilic and Dr. Benjamin Busam . Before that, I finished my Master and Bachelor study in National University of Defense Technology, China.

I am currently working in the field of 3D computer vision, particularly in geometric descriptors, shape & scene matching, pose estimation, and point cloud registration. Before that, I was active in the ACM International Collegiate Programming Contest (ACM-ICPC), where I was listed as the world final contestant for 2 times (2015 & 2016).
